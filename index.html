I am a PhD student at Scuola Normale Superiore, in Pisa, under the supervision of Roberto Frigerio.

My research interests include geometric group theory and nonpositive curvature. If you want to know more about my research, check out my papers and talks. If you don't, you can play Hex with a friend.

I have taken part in programming competitions at the highest levels, and have been a finalist in the International Olympiads in Informatics (IOI) and the International Collagiate Programming Contest (ICPC). I have also been a coach for some teams of students taking part in the ICPC.
